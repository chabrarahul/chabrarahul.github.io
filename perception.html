Understanding

Decoding the world, not just labeling it.

Traditional computer vision asks, "What is in this image?" We ask, "What is happening, and why?"

Our research moves beyond static recognition to temporal and causal reasoning. By modeling long-form narratives and physical dynamics, we enable machines to perceive the chaotic real world with human-level depth.

Hi! I am a second-year PhD student at the Purdue University in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am part of Convergence Design Lab, working with Professor Karthik Ramani. I received my Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University and Bachelor’s in Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Patna. My primary interest lies at intersection of computer vision, machine learning and human-computer interactions. .

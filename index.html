Hello! I’m a final-year PhD student at Purdue University and a member of the Convergence Design Lab, advised by Prof. Karthik Ramani. My research lies at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and human–AI interaction, with a focus on building physically intelligent and human-centered systems.

Ph.D. Student @ Purdue University

jain348 [at] purdue.edu

Hi! I am a Rahul Jain, a second-year PhD student at the Purdue University in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am part of Convergence Design Lab, working with Professor Karthik Ramani. I am also working with Murat Kocaoglu and Ryo Suzuki for various research projects. I received my Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University and Bachelor’s in Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna. My primary interest lies in intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and human-computer interactions.

AI/ML side: Development of ML models adapting to user needs and understanding.

Human Understanding: Exploring human embodied learning and environment.

Human AI Interaction : Designing interactions between Humans and AI.

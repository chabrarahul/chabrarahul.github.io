Ph.D. Student @ Purdue University

jain348 [at] purdue.edu

Hello! I am Rahul Jain, a third-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University. I am part of Convergence Design Lab, working with Professor Karthik Ramani. In addition, I collaborate with Murat Kocaoglu and Ryo Suzuki on various research projects. My academic journey includes a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Purdue University and a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna. My primary interest lies at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and human-computer interactions.

AI/ML: Developing ML models adapting to user needs and understanding.

Human Understanding: Exploring human embodied learning and environment.

Human AI Interaction: Designing interactions between Humans and AI.
